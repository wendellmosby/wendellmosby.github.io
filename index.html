About Wendell

Purpose is not a slogan. It is the through-line.

Wendell Mosby is an educator, founder, author, speaker, and community impact strategist whose work sits at the intersection of education, technology, health advocacy, and leadership.

From a childhood nickname that evolved into the life philosophy "Walking In My Purpose," Wendell has spent his career helping people and organizations turn challenges into opportunities for growth and impact.

Whether teaching 6th graders in Gary, Indiana, leading nonprofit initiatives, advocating for families affected by Type 1 diabetes, or exploring the role of AI in education, Wendell believes that purpose is most powerful when it serves others.
